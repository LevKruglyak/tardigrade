\subsection{Velocity Verlet Algorithm}

The velocity Verlet algorithm has iteration steps:
\[
  \begin{aligned}
    \xx(t+\Delta t) &= \xx(t) + \vv(t)\Delta t + \frac{1}{2}\aa(t)\Delta t^2\\
    \vv(t+\Delta t) &= \vv(t) + \frac{1}{2}(\aa(t) + \aa(t+\Delta t))\Delta t 
  \end{aligned}
\]
